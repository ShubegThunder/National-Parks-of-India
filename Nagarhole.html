🐘 Nagarhole National Park

Nagarhole National Park, also known as Rajiv Gandhi National Park, is located in Karnataka and is famed for its rich biodiversity, dense forests, and large herds of elephants and tigers.

📍 Location & Landscape

Situated in the Kodagu and Mysore districts of Karnataka, Nagarhole is part of the Nilgiri Biosphere Reserve. The park covers around 640 sq km and features moist deciduous forests, streams, hills, and valleys.

⚠️ Best Time to Visit

  • October to May: Ideal for wildlife sightings and safaris
  • June to September: Monsoon season; lush greenery but limited access

🚗 How to Reach

  • By Air: Mysore Airport (96 km), Bengaluru International Airport (236 km)
  • By Road: Well connected by road to Mysore, Madikeri, and Bangalore
  • By Train: Mysore Railway Station (around 90 km)

🦁 Wildlife & Activities

  • Jeep and bus safaris to spot elephants, tigers, leopards, and wild dogs
  • Birdwatching—home to over 270 species of birds
  • Nature photography and trekking along permitted trails

🎟️ Fees & Etiquette

  • Entry + safari: approx. ₹400–₹600 per person (varies by season)
  • Follow forest rules strictly—no plastic, no noise, and no feeding animals
