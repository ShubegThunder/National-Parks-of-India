🐃 Periyar National Park

Periyar National Park in Kerala is known for its scenic lake, diverse wildlife including elephants, tigers, and gaur, and rich tribal heritage.

📍 Location & Landscape

Set within the Western Ghats in Thekkady, Periyar spreads over 925 sq km. Its forested slopes, spice plantations, and Periyar Lake offer a serene ecosystem.

⚠️ Best Time to Visit

  • December to February: Pleasant weather and peak wildlife sighting season
  • March to May: Hot, but wildlife gathers near water

🚗 How to Reach

  • By Air: Kochi Airport (135 km)
  • By Road: Well-connected from Kochi and Madurai
  • By Train: Kottayam Railway Station (107 km)

🦣 Wildlife & Activities

  • Boat ride on Periyar Lake for elephants and birds
  • Nature walks with tribal guides
  • Spice plantation tours and bamboo rafting

🎟️ Fees & Etiquette

  • Entry + boat combo: approximately ₹500 per person (subject to season)
  • Respect local tribal communities and avoid plastic waste
