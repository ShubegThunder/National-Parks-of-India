🐾 Welcome to the National Parks of India

India boasts a stunning array of ecosystems and wildlife, making it a top destination for nature lovers and conservationists. With over 100 national parks scattered across the subcontinent, each protected area showcases the country's unique biodiversity and natural heritage.

🌿 Discover the Wild Side

Explore detailed guides to 30+ of India's most prominent national parks. From lush jungles to alpine meadows and arid deserts, each park has a distinct character. Learn about:

  • Iconic wildlife like Bengal tigers, Asiatic lions, elephants, and rhinos
  • Bird sanctuaries and endemic species unique to the Indian subcontinent
  • Seasonal insights, safari booking tips, and accommodation options
  • Eco-tourism and responsible travel practices to protect these treasures

🐅 Why They Matter

India's national parks are more than protected spaces — they are sanctuaries for life. They play a vital role in:

  • Conserving endangered and threatened species
  • Maintaining ecological balance and biodiversity
  • Generating livelihoods through sustainable tourism
  • Serving as outdoor classrooms for education and awareness

📌 Plan Your Journey

Whether you're planning a weekend escape or an in-depth wildlife photography trip, use this site to plan your visit. Each park's page includes:

  • How to reach, entry fees, and best visiting seasons
  • Detailed park maps and safari zones
  • Top flora and fauna to look out for
  • Local conservation efforts and ongoing challenges
